Who is BLACKPINK?

Blackpink is a record-breaking South Korean girl group featuring members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. Debuting in 2016, they became a global music phenomenon by fusing fierce hip-hop with catchy pop hooks. They are historic Coachella headliners and major luxury fashion icons who dominate the global charts.

J I S O O

Jisoo is Blackpink's lead vocalist who debuted with the group in 2016. She is celebrated for her husky vocals, her chart-topping solo hit "Flower," and her prominent acting roles. A massive style icon, she also serves as a premier global ambassador for Dior.

J E N N I E

Jennie is Blackpink's versatile main rapper known for her fierce stage presence and vocals. She pioneered the group's solo music with her hit "Solo" and runs her own label, Odd Atelier. She heavily influences the high-fashion world as a global ambassador for Chanel.

R O S É

Rosé is the Korean-New Zealand main vocalist of Blackpink, recognized for her distinct, emotional voice. She made history with her successful solo tracks and continues to release acclaimed individual music. She is also a prominent global ambassador for luxury powerhouses Saint Laurent and Tiffany & Co.

L I S A

Lisa is Blackpink's Thai main dancer, celebrated globally for her sharp choreography and rap verses. She broke multiple records with her solo music, runs her own company LLOUD, and acts in The White Lotus. She dominates high fashion as a global ambassador for Louis Vuitton.
